The opportunity
1
The insight
The Bay Area has some of the most wellness-conscious, high-income adults in the country — and most of them have already tried IV therapy. They love it. They just can't get it from someone they actually trust. The clinics feel corporate. The pop-ups are inconsistent. Nobody has built the version that feels like calling your NP best friend.
2
The gap
What's missing is a licensed, relationship-based provider who comes to you — and who you can text. That relationship doesn't exist at scale in the Bay Area yet.
3
The approach
Mobile IV pop-ups + on-call bookings, anchored by a monthly membership. Coco runs clinical. Kelly runs operations, partnerships, and growth. Lean overhead, high margins, recurring revenue from month one.
4
The proof
Coco's existing Oakland network already uses IV therapy and has been asking when she launches. Pre-validated demand before a dollar in marketing spend. The unit economics — 45 IVs/month at $300 average — mirror what's working for solo NP practices in LA, SF, and Denver right now.
5
The ask
$100K from 4–6 investors who already know the value of what we're building — because they've lived it. This buys six months of operating runway, full legal and clinical infrastructure, and the pop-up network to hit $10K/month by month five. In return: equity, service credits, and first access as we expand.
Growth roadmap
Phase 1
M1–3
Legal + soft launch. CA Professional Corp formed, supervising physician via medical director partner (Prax Health / Guardian), malpractice secured. Private beta: 10–20 IVs with Coco's warm network.
Phase 2
M3–5
Pop-up network + membership. 2–3 partner locations (pilates, sauna, wellness studios). Monthly IV membership live. Target: 20 members + 40 IVs/month = $10K+ MRR.
Phase 3
M5–9
GLP-1 + NAD programs launch. High-margin recurring subscription layer on top of IV base. Referral program drives organic growth from angel network outward.
Phase 4
M10+
Brick + mortar considered once MRR exceeds $15K consistently. Nevada (Incline Village) expansion evaluated at 25+ members. Aesthetic services added.
